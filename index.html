Our Story

Founded in 2015, JAPH Arch Company Limited began as a small architectural design studio with a clear mission: to blend innovative design with sound engineering principles. What started with a handful of passionate architects and engineers quickly grew into a multidisciplinary firm trusted for both design excellence and construction precision. In its early years, JAPH focused on residential and commercial architectural projects, earning recognition for creative yet functional building designs that suited both local climates and client needs. By 2018, the company expanded its services to include structural engineering and project management, allowing it to deliver complete solutions from concept to completion.
